Requirements

All applicants must meet every requirement listed below before submitting an application. Applications that do not meet these criteria will be automatically declined.

🎂

Age Requirement

You must be 16 years of age or older. We need our staff to be mature and handle situations responsibly.

⏱️

Server Tenure

You must have been a member of IslesOfDawnMC for at least 2 months, or have at least 2 weeks of total playtime logged on the server.

🛡️

Clean Punishment Record

You must not have received any warnings, mutes, bans, kicks, or other punishments within the past month. A history of repeated violations may lead to disqualification.

🎙️

Working Microphone

You must have a working microphone for staff meetings, coordination, and in-game communication with the team.

💬

English Proficiency

You must be able to communicate fluently in English, both written and spoken, to effectively coordinate with the team and support players.

📅

Consistent Availability

You must be able to dedicate a consistent and reasonable amount of time each week to fulfilling your role. Prolonged inactivity without notice may result in removal.

📋

Rule Knowledge

You must be thoroughly familiar with all IslesOfDawnMC server rules and actively uphold them. Staff are expected to lead by example at all times.

🤝

Professionalism

You must consistently demonstrate respectful, professional, and mature behaviour toward all players and fellow staff members, both on and off the server.
